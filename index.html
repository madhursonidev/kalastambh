Presenting Wildlife Photography Exhibition & Contest

Indore Tiger Festival 2025

by KalaStambh

Celebrate the spirit of wildlife and photography with Kalastambh
where every frame roars with passion!  

Theme: “The Tiger & The Wild”
Special priority will be given to photographs of tigers shot in Madhya Pradesh’s tiger reserves, including Kanha, Bandhavgarh, Pench, Panna, and Satpura. Other exceptional wildlife entries are also welcome from all over India.

Exhibition Dates
: 28 July - 15 August 2025
Award Ceremony
: 29 July 2025
Where
: Phoenix Citadel Mall, Indore
Time
: 11 AM to 8 PM
Last Date of Submission
: 15 July 2025

Eligibility:
• Open to amateur and professional photographers across India.
• Participants must be 16 years or older.
• Entry Fee: ₹1000 per picture

Submit Now!

Submission Process:
fill the above form:
• Upload up to 4 photographs (.jpg/.jpeg)
• File size: 10MB to 30MB
• Minimum resolution: 300 DPI
• Rename files as: YourName1, YourName2, etc.

Required with each photo:
• Title or caption
• Location taken
• Camera model and settings (optional)

Editing & Authenticity Guidelines:
Allowed: Basic edits like brightness, contrast, color balance, cropping
Not Allowed: Adding/removing objects, AI-generated images, collages, watermarked photos
Images must be original and unaltered in subject matter.

Judging & Exhibition:
Entries will be evaluated by a panel of wildlife photography experts. Selected photos will be exhibited at Phoenix Citadel Mall, Indore.
